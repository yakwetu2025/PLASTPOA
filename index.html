Communication APIs

Integrating APIs for real-time tracking, instant updates, and interaction between drivers, dispatchers, and customers via WhatsApp and SMS. This fosters transparency and enhances communication throughout the delivery process, leading to improved customer satisfaction.

Facilitating secure and efficient transactions through integrated payment gateways, ensuring seamless financial operations and enhancing customer trust. This streamlined payment process provides a secure and convenient experience for all parties involved.
